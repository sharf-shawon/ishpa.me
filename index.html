F.A.Qs

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What services do you offer as a makeup artist?
    I offer a variety of makeup services, including bridal makeup, editorial makeup, special effects makeup, and more. Please visit my Services page to learn more.
  • What types of makeup do you specialize in?
    I specialize in a range of makeup styles, including natural makeup, glamour makeup, and artistic and creative makeup. I'm always happy to discuss your preferences and create a customized makeup look just for you.
  • Do you offer bridal makeup services?
    Yes, I offer bridal makeup services, including bridal trials and on-location services on the wedding day. Please visit my Bridal Makeup page for more information.
  • What is your pricing for makeup services?
    My pricing for makeup services varies depending on the specific service requested, such as bridal makeup, editorial makeup, or special effects makeup. Please visit my Pricing page to see more information.
  • Can you provide makeup services on location?
    Yes, I provide makeup services on location for events or photo shoots.
  • Do you use cruelty-free or vegan makeup products?
    Yes, I use a range of makeup products, including cruelty-free and vegan options. Please let me know if you have any specific preferences.
  • How long does it typically take to complete a makeup application?
    The amount of time required for a makeup application can vary depending on the specific look requested and the individual client. I typically allow 45 minutes to an hour for a full makeup application.
  • What is your experience as a makeup artist?
    I have several years of experience as a makeup artist, with a background in bridal, editorial, and creative makeup. Please visit my About page to learn more.
  • How can I schedule a makeup appointment with you?
    You can schedule a makeup appointment by contacting me via my website or email. To book your appointment a 25% advance deposit (refundable) is required.
  • What should I expect during a makeup appointment with you?
    During a makeup appointment, I will consult with you about your preferences and any specific requests you may have. I will then create your desired makeup look, ensuring your satisfaction before finishing the appointment.
  • Do you offer group makeup services?
    Yes, I offer group makeup services for events such as weddings or photo shoots. Please contact me for more information.
  • Can you recommend products for me to use for touch-ups throughout the day or evening?
    Yes, I am happy to recommend specific products for touch-ups throughout the day or evening, based on your skin type and the makeup products used.
  • What types of payment do you accept?
    I accept several forms of payment, including cash, CashApp, Venmo.
  • What is your cancellation policy for makeup services?
    I really appreciate if you let me as early as possible. Please let me know at least 24 hours ahead for a full refund of your deposit money.
